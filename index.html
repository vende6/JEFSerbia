About JEF Serbia

JEF Serbia is part of the Young European Federalists, a supranational political movement active in most European countries. We advocate for a federal Europe, democratic institutions, and European integration.

Our mission is to promote European federalism among young people, organize debates and events, and contribute to building a more united and democratic Europe through active participation and engagement.
